Satay Recipe and Cooking Guide

A Plate of Satay Served with a Sides of Sambal and Salad

Recipe Description

This is an original Indonesian satay. I send it from a long way. Hope all over the world, readers can enjoy it.

Ingredients

Pempek Ikan/"Fishcake"

  • Soy Sauce
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chicken Breast
  • Ground Cumin
  • Peanuts

Steps

  1. Prepare Peanuts Sauce
  2. Cut Chicken, Thread into Skewers
  3. Lightly grill the chicken
